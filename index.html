Welcome to Lab Safety 101! This website is designed to provide you with the basic knowledge of lab safety and the different techniques and equipment used to ensure safety in the lab.

Lab safety is important to ensure the well-being of everyone in the lab. It is important to know the different types of safety equipment and how to use them properly. This safety guide is not just important in the lab, but in other fields of work as well and day-to-day life.
